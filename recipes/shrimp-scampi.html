Shrimp Scampi with Angel Hair Pasta

Do you love seafood? How about pasta? If so, try making some shrimp scampi with angel hair pasta! Filled with soft but bursting flavors, it is guaranteed to be a hit on date night or loved by the entire familyâ€”even picky eaters! Simply prepare this step-by-step meal, and prepare to be amazed!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of angel hair pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 lemon, zested
  • 1/2 cup of dry white wine
  • 5 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parley leaves

Steps for Angel Hair Pasta

  1. Add pasta to a large pot of boiling salted water over medium heat. Cook until al dente.

Steps for Shrimp

  1. Meanwhile, heat a large 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add olive oil to skillet.
  3. Once skillet is shimmering and hot, add the shrimp and saute until just about cooked through for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Remove shrimp to a plate and reserve.
  5. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes to the skillet and, for about a minute, saute until garlic is fragrant.
  6. Add lemon juice and white wine to the skillet, and raise the heat to high.
  7. Let the liquid reduce for 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Whisk in the butter.
  9. Add a ladleful of the pasta cooking water and the shrimp.
  10. Remove the skillet from the heat.

Finishing Steps

  1. Drain the pasta in a colander.
  2. Add the pasta to the skillet with the chopped parsley. Toss.
  3. Add the lemon zest, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately.
