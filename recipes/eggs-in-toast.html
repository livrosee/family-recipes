Egg in a Hole

A delicious egg cooked over-easy inside the hole of a piece of toast!

Sick of the typical scrambled or over-easy eggs? The eggs-in-toast is a wonderful eggs-based breakfast to try! It combines both great breakfast foods—eggs and toast—into one tasty, quick-to-make (and eat!) meal. Just grab your eggs from the fridge, your sliced bread from the cupboard, and your spices from the spice rack, and you're practically set! Simply follow this easy recipe and get ready to eat a delicious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 slice of bread, any kind
  • table salt
  • ground black pepper
