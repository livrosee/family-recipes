Three Bean Salad

A beautiful, fresh three bean salad!

Looking for a healthy meal? Scrambling for a great dish to bring to a dinner or bbq? This Mexican three bean salad is a perfect meal! Filled with protein and bursting flavors, this dish is a lover to health-foodies and always a hit at family get-togethers. Pull together your favorite beans and get ready to make the most beautiful meal!

Ingredients for Bean Salad

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of great northern beans or cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 1/2 ounces of bagged frozen corn or 2 to 3 ears of fresh corn
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeño or serrano chili, seeded and diced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 to 2 avocados, diced

Ingredients for the Citrus Vinaigrette

  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/4 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ancho chili powder

Steps to Prepare Corn (on Grill)

  1. Shuck and clean the corn.
  2. Heat grill to medium hot.
  3. Place corn directly on grill, turn every ten minutes or until charred.

Steps to Prepare Frozen Corn (on skillet)

  1. Heat ten-inch iron skillet on medium hot heat.
  2. When hot, add corn kernels in a single layer and cook for three to four minutes.
  3. Stir and cook until corn is charred.
  4. Repeat until all corn is nicely charred.

Steps to Prepare Frozen Corn (as is)

  1. Defrost corn and use it as is.

Steps to Make the Citrus Vinaigrette

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together oil, lime juice, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, honey, mined garlic, ground cumin, salt, pepper, chili powder, and cilantro.

Assemble the Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the corn, beans, bell pepper, onions, and jalapeño.
  2. Pour the vinaigrette over the beans and veggies. Mix well to combine.
  3. Refrigerate the mix for a few hours, and add the avocado before serving.
